Utah Games

Here you will find games currently in development or recently released by Utah game developers. If you are a developer in Utah interested in promoting your game check out our forums.

Kaiju Stomp

Vince Betteridge

Kaiju Stomp is a board game for up to four players. Each player is a giant monster that jumps around a city in search of power crystals. Be the first to collect all your power crystals and bring them to your reactor to become a supreme beast. Once you are supreme you must stomp on your enemies before they also become supreme or you enter sudden elimination.

vincebetteridge.blogspot.com

Magnetic By Nature

Triple Slash Studios

Magnetic By Nature is platforming without platforms, a 2D action-puzzler for Windows, Mac, and Linux. Unique magnet physics propel you through a stylish netherworld of vivid color and constant danger. As the last living automaton, you must rappel from rolling magnets, catapult across chasms, and slingshot through space to reactivate long-lost friends.

www.magneticbynature.com

Together

Mount Olympus Games

Rather than tacking on a second player to a single player campaign, Together is an adventure puzzle game built from the ground up to be an interdependent co-operative experience. It is a great game to play with your friends or loved ones. Sign up for the newsletter to be notified when the game goes to kickstarter or is released here.
